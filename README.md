# uni-print-service
Welcome to our HCMUT_SPSS

## Overview
HCMUT_SPSS is a smart printing service for HCMUT students. It allows students to submit print jobs, manage printer queues, and handle printing credits. Students can submit print jobs from any device, and the system will handle the rest. Students can also manage their printing credits and print jobs. Students can also view their print history and print job details.

The system is managed by Student Printing Service Office (SPSO). They are responsible for managing the printers and the printing credits. SPSO can also see the print history and print job details of all students.
